About Siquijor

The Island of Fire

Siquijor is a small island province in the Philippines known for its pre-colonial traditions and healing practices. It is a place where the ocean meets the jungle, offering a tranquility that is hard to find elsewhere.

From the luminous waterfalls to the ancient banyan trees, every corner tells a story. Our luxury retreats are designed to help you experience the authentic beauty of this mystical island.
